💡 Smart Energy Tips

🌡️

Adjust AC Temperature

Setting your AC to 24°C instead of 20°C can save up to 20% on cooling costs.

💡

Switch to LED Bulbs

LED bulbs use 75% less energy and last 25x longer than incandescent lights.

🔌

Unplug Standby Devices

Electronics on standby can account for up to 10% of your electricity bill.

📅

Schedule High-Load Tasks

Move cycles like laundry and dishwashing to off-peak hours to reduce peak demand.

⚖️

Balance Load Distribution

Distribute energy use more evenly across dayparts to improve efficiency.

🧠

Review Device Scheduling

Optimizing run times for high-usage devices can deliver immediate savings.

📈

Compare Weekly Trends

Track progress week over week to identify consistent savings or spikes.

🧾

Audit High-Usage Devices

Review the top consumers and check if any can be replaced or rescheduled.

🕒

Reduce Idle Runtime

Switch off devices when not in use instead of leaving them in standby mode.

🔧

Set Operational Goals

Use a weekly reduction target to keep your energy strategy focused.

Category heat map

Advanced Optimization Strategies

Peak demand reduction

Cap simultaneous appliance use during peak hours to avoid rate surcharges.

Seasonal adjustment

Efficiency benchmarking

Device lifecycle planning

Track old appliances and plan replacement timing to upgrade to more efficient models.

Behavioral nudging

Set weekly reduction targets and celebrate wins to build sustainable energy habits.

Rate arbitrage
